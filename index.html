What LSDH Do?

Our lab is part of the
Medical Biodynamics Center (MBC).
  • We cultivate an open and collaborative research community.
  • We strive to unravel the secrets of successful aging through cross-disciplinary research.
  • We delve into the nuances of cognitive aging and resilience.
  • We ensure our work resonates with and serves those most impacted by accelerated aging.
